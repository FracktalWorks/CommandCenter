  • 1Outcome first — why this block matters sits above what it is. Busywork never gets this line; the absence is a signal too.
  • 2Pomodoro or Flow — cycles with dots, or a count-up flow timer for deep work; either way actuals feed learned estimates.
  • 3Subtasks tick off in place — no leaving the room. A batch block plays here as a rapid-fire queue.
  • 4Guilt-free +15 — overrunning shifts the flexible rest-of-day automatically. "Done early" is celebrated and logged.
  • 5Peripheral vision, one line — next break, next block. Enough context to stay calm, not enough to distract.
