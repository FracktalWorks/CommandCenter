practices. Verdict per area ‚Äî **=SOTA** (matches/exceeds the documented best

**Bottom line:** on the **agent loop, streaming/event protocol, HITL, long-running + multi-worker correctness, loop/stall detection, and now prompt caching** ‚Äî the A1/A2/A3/A5 core plus C4 ‚Äî CommandCenter is **at or above the documented state of the art**, and unusually, the harness itself is locked by CI-blocking trajectory evals (a practice the list preaches but few implement). None are correctness bugs; all are scheduled in the review order. The top cost lever (prompt caching) is closed at the mechanism level ‚Äî realised savings scale with Anthropic/OpenAI traffic vs the default DeepSeek (which self-caches).
